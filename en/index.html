Hi, I'm Tor.

What I love most is people, processes, and stories. I work with managers and organizations who understand that you go further together. I also have a day job as a product manager at a large tech company, a podcast called Weekly Sync, and I'm a single mom to Neri, so yes, I also run time management workshops.

What I Actually Do

I meet with managers who have a tangle of thoughts in their head, and help them leave the meeting with everything clean, organized, and ready for action.

I come into organizations to understand what's stuck, and together we build a plan to do things differently – one that doesn't just look good on paper, but one that people actually want to execute.

What Guides Me

I believe in combining the practical with the personal. Not "either-or".
I teach time management, but really I teach life management.
I look at things with a kind eye, but also a very sharp one.

I'm here to help you do more – better, easier, more enjoyably. No pomposity, no inflated words – just making things work better, and bringing the spark back to the doing.
