Organizations

I work with managers who want to do things differently. Whether it's building a clear strategy, taking an honest look at the management culture that grew on its own and changing it, or navigating change and burnout – I'm here because it's always easier together.

How We Can Work Together

Manager Coaching (1:1)

A space to get clarity and have someone in your corner. Especially good for transitions: stepping into a first management role or a senior one, leading a complex project, or figuring out what's next.

The goal isn't just to solve the current problem – it's to build a quiet, steady confidence as a manager. Together we'll cut through the noise, build an action plan, and reconnect the effective how with your professional and personal why.

Strategy, For Real – Organizational Work

I come into organizations to understand what's really stuck, and build a work plan that people actually want to execute.

Through analysis and working through problems together, we get the team on board with meaning, smart internal communication, and serious follow-through that keeps the healthy tension of actually getting things done.

Time & Task Management: Workshops and Team Coaching

They call it "time management," but there's no such thing – in practice we learn to manage attention, energy, and availability. It's far less mumbo jumbo than you've been told.

The real change happens when you work as a team: creating shared language about calendars, redefining what's actually urgent, running meetings that work, and protecting space for Deep Work that delivers.

Available as a team workshop, a one-time talk, or personal coaching for managers who want to take back control of their calendar.

Stress-Free Time Management

Time management is a life skill. We all juggle roles, commitments, dreams, and tasks – all in 24 hours. In this workshop we learn practical approaches and get tools to find what actually works for us. Time passes whether we manage it or not – so we learn to manage ourselves and our environment, and get more done, calmly.

We'll build a system that fits your priorities, learn to prioritize, carve out distraction-free time, and develop habits for calm, efficient organization – adapted to each person's actual life.

What we do in the workshop

  • Break myths about time management
  • Build a personal self-management system that actually works
  • Learn to prioritize and define availability and urgency
  • Learn to lift our head from the urgent and execute what's important
